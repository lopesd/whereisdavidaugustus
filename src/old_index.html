It's been too long since I've done one of these. Let's recap...

I was just about ready to go to the San Juan Islands up north when my motorcycle battery died. I jump-started it a bunch, eventually got it fully replaced. Then I realized my clutch was burnt, so I took her to the shop. Then when I was ready to go, a huge plume of smoke coming from the wildfires in California and Eastern WA covered the PNW, making the air quality very unhealthy and visibility low.

I wonder what creative impediment awaits next time I try to leave. Is someone trying to tell me something?

Still, hopes are high and I haven't abandoned my plans in the slightest. The Great Escape is on, bitch.
